### Key Takeaways

- Prefer memory-safe languages.
- In Java, do not use the `Unsafe` classes. Prefer the FFM API.
- Use SAST tools to detect and review occurrences, present or future.
- Use SCA tools to keep your third-party dependencies free from CVEs.

Note: Applies to choosinng or writing an application.
