### Why do people use it?

A study <sup>[5]</sup> about `sun.misc.Unsafe` found that there are two
main reasons why developer use it:

1. Performance
2. Advice on Stack Overflow

Note: But do you really need the performance gain? Have you measured
it? Donald Knuth famously said: "Premature optimization is the root of
all evil".
